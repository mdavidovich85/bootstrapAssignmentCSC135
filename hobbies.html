Cooking 20+ I love to cook. I started baking as a child and watched plenty of Food Network in middle-school and high school. In college I started getting more creative about cooking, but it wasn't until I graduated that I learned more about nutrition and buying and cooking good, wholesome food. I subscribe to Food & Wine magazine and currently enjoy learning new recipes from New Your Times Cooking. New York Times Cooking
Fitness 10+ My father died from cancer and I truly believe that his life could have been better if he had simply taken better care of himself through fitness and exercise. As a former "fat kid," as well, I've always been sensitive about my weight but always remind myself that working out is for my health and not to achieve an unachievable body. I enjoy weight lifting, biking, tennis, and indoor rock climbing. I'm currently a member of Lifetime Fitness. Lifetime Fitness

It's unfortunately a very expensive hobby, so my progress with it has been limited. As I invest in and complete school, I will have to put this hobby on hold for the time being. I model in O Scale.
