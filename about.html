About Me

After receiving my bachelor's degree in theatre from The University of New Hampshire, I had a five year career in theatre, acting, teaching and music directing. Theatre was a passion, but it was a difficult path to continue.

While the theatre has given me skills and perspective that I don't think I could have developed anywhere else, and while my business career has been successful, I'm interested in the hands on challenges and real-world problem solving that computer science and the related careers have to offer.

I have two years of course work to complete before I receive my B.S. in Computer Science and though I have much to learn about what the field has to offer, programming and software development interest me as well as working with clients to develop IT business solutions.
